## ğŸ“ˆ Analytics Features

### Hospital Operations Analytics
- **Patient Metrics**: Registration trends, demographics, visit frequency
- **Appointment Analytics**: Scheduling patterns, no-show rates, doctor utilization
- **Medical Records**: Diagnosis trends, treatment outcomes, record completion
- **Lab Tests**: Test volume, turnaround times, result patterns

### Financial Analytics
- **Revenue Tracking**: Appointment revenue, service billing
- **Cost Analysis**: Resource utilization, staff efficiency
- **Trend Analysis**: Monthly/quarterly comparisons
- **Forecasting**: Predictive analytics for planning

### Operational Analytics
- **Staff Performance**: Doctor productivity, appointment completion rates
- **Resource Utilization**: Room usage, equipment utilization
- **Quality Metrics**: Patient satisfaction, treatment success rates
- **Efficiency Metrics**: Wait times, processing times

### Custom Reports
- **Ad-hoc Reports**: Custom queries and filters
- **Scheduled Reports**: Automated report generation
- **Export Options**: PDF, CSV, Excel formats
- **Distribution**: Email delivery, dashboard sharing
